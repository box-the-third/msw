My Wellness Program

It is the most unique program in Saudi Arabia dedicated to changing the lifestyles of the participants and their community to become more healthy and is the most comprehensive wellness program in the Middle East.

The program empowers the participant to improve their wellness by developing healthy living habits so as to remain well and vibrant in both their professional and personal life.

My Saudi Wellness aims to:

  • Increase employee productivity and reduce sick leave days
  • Enhance recruitment, retention and employee satisfaction
  • Decrease work related injuries

Our Package

Various packages are available to meet the needs of our clients and can be tailored to specific objectives. These packages will include a various combination of the following:

Wellness Assessment
Portal

This private personalized online tool was designed to allow each participant to follow their own changes and includes:

  • Comprehensive health assessment for the participant
  • Daily news and recommendations
  • Nutritional advice for a more well life
  • Online fitness guides
  • Health age calculator to follow the participants' changes
  • Online tracking of rewards, and challenges

Wellness
Street

By walking through our "wellness street" participants will be assessed by our trained professionals who will do a range of assessments including:

  • A basic Medical exam
  • A range of Laboratory tests
  • Heart test
  • Stress assessment
  • A unique fitness test performed by our fitness professionals
  • Body fat and metabolic rates

Analysis &
Mentoring

Participants receive a wellness report covering five primary components. This is an accurate indicator of the participants' current health and wellness and is the best predictor of long term health and wellness.

Mentoring will aim to maintaining engagement and building commitment from participants to reach their goals by online monitoring, seminars, challenges and rewards as well as personal interviews.
